Humans [and/as] Gods is a speculation and critique on humanity and our perspectives toward innovation, history, advance, and religion/godhood.

As humans utilize science, we began playing “gods,” fueled by greed and the need for power and control, bending the world to our whims—we’ve workshopped natural and unnatural processes to fit exactly what we desire.

Like “Operation Sam,” by Hasbro Entertainment, each piece inserted into Sam represents an aspect of “godhood” that we humans have forcibly inserted into our lives and environments.
